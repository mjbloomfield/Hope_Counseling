### Biggest Competitive Insights

**The RMCC neighborhood strategy:** Rocky Mountain Counseling Collective embeds a list of Denver neighborhoods into EVERY service page title/description: *"Jefferson Park, Highland, Sloan's Lake, Sunnyside, Berkeley, West Highland, Chaffee Park, LoDo, Five Points, Baker, Capitol Hill, Cherry Creek, Washington Park, City Park."* HCFT should do the equivalent for Boulder neighborhoods + Denver suburbs.

**Midlife therapy is a low-competition goldmine:** Only 1 competitor (My Denver Therapy) has a `/midlife-therapy-counseling-denver-co/` page. HCFT's entire brand is built on this specialty. This is the highest-opportunity SEO page in the site.

**Telehealth pages are universal:** All 13 competitors have dedicated telehealth content. HCFT's contact page covers this but a standalone `/telehealth-therapy-colorado.html` page would capture "online therapy Denver CO" searches.

**RLT is almost unclaimed:** Only Colorado Relationship Recovery uses RLT. Neither they nor HCFT own a dedicated `/relational-life-therapy-denver/` page yet.

1. **Midlife therapy Denver** — 1 competitor page, HCFT's primary specialty ✅ built
