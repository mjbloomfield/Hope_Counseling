Coaching · Men in midlife · Worldwide

Men's Midlife Coaching

Forward-focused work for men reckoning with career, relationship, identity, and the question of what's next. Online from anywhere.

Coaching for the Man in the Reckoning

Most men I coach aren't in crisis. They're stuck in a chapter that no longer fits and they don't yet know what the next one looks like. Coaching is a structured space to figure that out and start moving. Without the cliches and without being told what to want.

What Men Bring to Coaching

Career questioning, job loss, retirement

The work that defined you isn't working anymore, or it's about to end. We design what comes next, both practically and identity-wise.

Relationship distress, loss of passion, disconnection

For men working forward on a partnership. Wanting to deepen, redesign, or get clarity. (For active distress or repair, look at couples therapy.)

Parenting, launching children, empty-nesting

The shift from being needed to being something else. What do you want fatherhood to look like in this next phase?

Divorce, breakups, "dating while gray"

Forward design after the end of a marriage. Building a self that isn't defined by the role you just left.

Sexual dissatisfaction and dysfunction

Underdiscussed, underaddressed. A coaching space to bring it into language and design what you want from this part of life.

Caring for elders, family and financial concerns

Strategic clarity on the middle squeeze, and a life design that doesn't collapse under it.

Identity, purpose, legacy

What do you want this chapter to be about? What do you want to have built? Coaching is a place to take that question seriously without it staying abstract.

Coaching is worldwide. Online from anywhere. Most clients meet weekly or every other week. If you're in Colorado and what you actually need is therapy. See men's therapy.
