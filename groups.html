Community & Connection

Therapy Support Groups in Boulder, CO

Small, facilitated support groups that offer connection, shared wisdom, and community alongside individual therapy work.

Why Group Therapy

The Power of Being Truly Understood

Support groups offer something individual therapy cannot: the experience of being witnessed and understood by others who are navigating similar terrain. Hearing "me too" from someone who genuinely means it is uniquely healing.

🪺
Currently Offered

Empty Nest Support Group

This co-ed support group is for anyone who is preparing to become an "empty nester" or has recently experienced their last child leaving home. Empty nesting is one of the most significant yet underacknowledged transitions of midlife — and it deserves real community and real conversation.

Empty nesting brings a complex mix of emotions that often coexist uncomfortably: sadness, loss of purpose, anxiety, loneliness — alongside pride, joy, relief, and a sense of accomplishment. Many people feel they "shouldn't" be struggling with something that is supposed to be a positive milestone. The group makes space for all of it.

What We Do in the Group

  • Connect with others who genuinely understand this life phase
  • Explore the complex emotions of this transition without judgment
  • Discuss identity: who are you now, outside the daily parenting role?
  • Address relationship changes — with your partner, with your kids, with yourself
  • Share strategies that help and learn from others doing the same work

Grief Support

4-Week Interactive Bereavement Support Group

This intimate grief group uses thought-provoking tools and creative projects to help members express their feelings, find meaning, and begin the transition into life after loss. It's not a lecture series or a place where everyone simply tells their story. It's an active, participatory process designed to move grief from isolation into community.

Grief groups are run based on community need and enrollment. Call to inquire about the next scheduled group or to be placed on the interest list.

About Group Facilitation

What to Expect From a Group Facilitated by D. Hope Tola

Groups at Hope Counseling are small, carefully facilitated, and built on the same values that guide all of my clinical work: honesty, directness, and genuine care.

🤝

Small & Intimate

Groups are kept small intentionally — enough participants to create community, few enough that no one disappears into the background.

🧭

Structured, Not Unguided

Each session has a clear structure and purpose. You won't simply sit in a circle and take turns venting. The format is active and facilitated.

🌱

Complementary to Individual Work

Groups work best alongside individual therapy — or as a starting point for those exploring therapy for the first time. They're not a replacement for individual care.
