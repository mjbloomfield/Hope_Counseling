## 3. On-Page SEO — Site-Wide Summary

### Title Tags
[Confirmed findings]:
- **Pattern:** All confirmed titles follow "[Page Name] \| HCFT, PLLC" except the homepage which uses a descriptive title with keywords ("Couples and Midlife Specialty").
- **Issue:** Interior pages use the generic brand abbreviation "HCFT, PLLC" rather than the full brand name or keyword-rich suffix. "HCFT" is not a recognized term; searchers would not know this abbreviation.
- **Homepage title is the strongest:** "Hope Counseling & Family Therapy | Couples and Midlife Specialty" — includes relevant keywords.
- **Missed opportunities:** Service pages like "/relationships," "/individuals," "/grief-counseling," and "/outdoor-therapy" likely have generic titles. The confirmed title for /about-6 is "Midlife Coaching | HCFT, PLLC" — correct keyword but wrong URL.

[Not confirmed on any page] — No meta descriptions were found in the static HTML of any page fetched. This is either a Wix rendering issue (meta tags injected after JavaScript loads, invisible to crawlers) or meta descriptions are absent entirely. Given Wix typically renders meta tags server-side, the most likely explanation is that meta descriptions have not been set for most pages. This is a significant gap: Google will auto-generate snippets, but they will be less compelling than crafted descriptions.
