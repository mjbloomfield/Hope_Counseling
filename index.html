Hi, I'm Hope

Honest. Direct. Caring.

I work with couples, with men and women navigating midlife, and with the relationships that hold a family together. I bring real engagement — not platitudes, not a one-size-fits-all formula.

I draw on the Gottman Method, Relational Life Therapy, IFS, EMDR, somatic approaches, and positive psychology in therapy. In coaching, I bring the same psychological grounding to forward-focused goal work — career changes, midlife reinvention, parenting transitions, and the chapter you're trying to step into.
