Hi, I'm Hope

Real engagement, not platitudes

My practice is built around couples, around men and women in midlife, and around the family relationships that shape a life. I'm not interested in vague reassurance or one-size-fits-all advice; what I offer is honest, careful attention to whatever you're actually carrying.

In therapy I work primarily within the Gottman Method and Relational Life Therapy, with additional training in positive psychology, play therapy with couples and families, and specialized grief and bereavement work. In coaching I bring that same psychological grounding to forward-focused work on career change, midlife reinvention, parenting transitions, and the bigger questions about what you want the next stretch of your life to look like.

Men & Women in Midlife

Midlife often arrives with the ground shifting underfoot, whether through divorce, a career reckoning, sexual or health changes, or the slower question of who you're becoming. Therapy is the right container when there's grief or stuckness to work through; coaching is the right one when you know roughly where you're going and want help getting there.
