The Real Cost of Therapy

Therapy and coaching are investments in your mental health, your relationships, and your quality of life. Consider what poor mental health, a struggling relationship, or a stalled transition costs you. In time, energy, wellbeing, and even financial decisions made from a difficult mental state. Viewed this way, effective work often pays dividends well beyond its direct cost.
