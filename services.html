What I Offer

Therapy & Counseling Services in Boulder & Denver, CO

A full range of evidence-based therapy services for adults, couples, families, and groups — in person in Boulder and via telehealth throughout Colorado.

Core Services

Individual, Couples & Family Therapy

Each service is informed by the same values: honesty, directness, care, and solutions-focused engagement with where you are and where you want to go.

💑

Couples Therapy

Evidence-based couples counseling using the Gottman Method, Relational Life Therapy (RLT), and Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT). For couples navigating conflict, disconnection, infidelity recovery, major life transitions, or premarital preparation.

Gottman MethodRLTEFT

Midlife Therapy Primary Specialty

Specialized therapy for adults ages 35–65 navigating identity shifts, empty nest, career transitions, divorce, aging, and the question of what comes next. This is our primary specialty and a deeply underserved population.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one therapy for anxiety, grief, burnout, identity questions, life transitions, relationship patterns, and more. Adults only, with a specialty in the midlife years (35–65). EMDR, IFS, somatic, and mindfulness-informed.

EMDRIFSSomatic

Family Therapy

Family sessions designed for parent–teen pairs and family configurations experiencing communication breakdowns, conflict, or adjustment challenges. Structured for the pair, not just the "identified" child.

Grief Counseling

Compassionate grief therapy for loss of all kinds: death, divorce, identity, health, relationships. Individual grief counseling and facilitated group support. EMDR and somatic approaches for complicated grief.
